Digital Analytics and Insights

Wondering how to bring your business to the digital landscape? How to build your digital presence and use it to your advantage, reach out to me! I help business realise their digital potential by helping them with crunching the numbers. I am a certified Google Analytics Individual and have over 8 years of experience working with Data and analytics across different domain such as E-commerce, Marketing, Operations and Social Media.

Build/Integrate analytics capabilities into your website.

With a certification in Google Analytics, Google Adwords and Google Tag Manager, I can help provide a turn key solution to your website to get data and insights about your customers flowing to you.

Analyse your audience

Already have the tools to analyse audience? I can help you get started with asking the right questions and using data to find these answers. As always, the details lie in the data and you can use it to increase your business performance with marginal changes or understanding your audience.

Generate Insights and Recommendations

With a Master degree in Applied Information Systems, I have spent years wrangling data and crunching numbers to unearth the hidden insights behind the numbers. I can help you build reporting solutions that can show market trends and your audiences pattern. I can also help build and test recommendations for your next steps to achieve your digital goal.

Social Media Marketing

If your audience isn’t getting to you, you get to them! I have spent over 6 years in helping small to medium businesses manage their advertising spend and budget optimisation across social media and search engines. I am experienced in a variety of Online advertising platforms such as Google, Bing Ads, Facebook and Instagram.

Defining your advertising strategy and goal

There are numerous ways in which you can get your audience’s attention, I can help you build your strategy and goal on what you should do when you get their attention and how. I am adept in defining advertising strategy and goals across multiple marketing platform that follow different models such as Display advertising, Pay Per click, Retargeting or Social Media advertising.

Target your audience

Answering your advertising goals raises questions on who to target. There are many options and ways to do that. I can help you with installing tools such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel and Google Tag Manager etc that gives you detailed insights to know and target your audience.

Test your hypothesis

Any decisions and insights based on a theory needs rigorous testing. I can help you set up A/B Testing to ensure that we are making our money work in the best possible way. A few dollars/euros/pounds spend on testing goes a long way when you make the big move and drop the chips on your marketing spend.

Measure and test again

With a masters degree in Statistics and applied Information systems, I can help measure and crunch the insights from your marketing ad spend to ensure that our money is spend at the right place, in the right way and at the right time.

Search Engine Optimisation

Did you know that you can significantly reduce your advertising budget by just doing small changes in your website for free? I can help you find these changes and also suggest how to improve your website’s popularity across popular search engines (Google, Bing, Yahoo etc.). With a certification in Google Digital Marketing, I can help you carry a thorough SEO analysis for your website including the health and customer sentiments for each page.

Establishing your SEO growth strategy

Most businesses fail to get free traffic on their website because they don’t have a well structured strategy on what is the final outcome of all that they are doing on their website. I can help you structure a growth strategy to drive traffic on your website without paying any money. Don’t just buy traffic, build content and use free strategies to get your business to people rather than driving them to you.

Defining your SEO goals

Once established, I can help breakdown your strategy into tangible goals/milestones. It is important to identify and set realistic SEO goals for your business. Eg: Do you want more conversions or more acquisitions on your website?

Keywords Research and Market analysis

I can help you choose the right keywords and text needed for search engines to prefer your website over your competitors. I leverage on tools such as Google Keywords Planner, Google Search Console and Google Webmaster to find the right keywords and therefore the right content that is most frequently searched, most relevant to your business and beats your competition.

Website Optimisation

Did you know that a well optimised website is like a Red Ferrari that needs no promotion. With a certification in Google Digital Marketing, I can help you increase your website’s visibility by optimising your pages, just the way search engines algorithms like.

A big factor towards increasing your visibility is also to enable back linking of your content on other website and leveraging the power of social media to keep your audience engaged and talking about you.
